Why is there a limit?

Every project deployed on LiteStack requires global edge propagation, storage, and computing power. To keep this service free for everyone without ads or selling data, we must manage our infrastructure costs carefully. Adding a 3rd project to every user would exponentially increase our overhead.

What can I do?

If you need to start a new project, you can delete one of your existing unused projects from the dashboard. This frees up resources for your new creation while ensuring we can keep LiteStack running for the entire community.
